About & safety

Crowd Light Show is a free, open-source web app that turns a crowd's phones into one light show synchronized to the music.

⚠️ Photosensitivity

The show flashes bright light. People with photosensitive epilepsy or sensitivity to flashing light should not take part. Flash rate is capped to no more than 3 flashes per second and large saturated-red strobing is blocked (per WCAG 2.3.1/2.3.2), but please use judgement. You can leave any time with the “Stop” button.

Privacy

Taking part in a show is anonymous — no account, no name, no email, and the join/show page sets no analytics cookies. If you enable the flashlight on Android, the browser asks for camera permission because that is how the web exposes the LED; no image is ever opened, recorded or sent — the camera is used only to switch the light on and off. Session data is discarded when the show ends. The marketing website itself uses Google Analytics only if you accept the cookie banner — see the privacy notice for details.
