English (authoritative)

Who is the controller

The data controller is Andrii Shramko (details in the imprint above). Contact for any privacy request: zmei116@gmail.com.

What we collect and why

When you submit the contact form we collect your name and email (required) and, if you choose, your phone, company, event type and message. We use them solely to reply to your enquiry and scope a possible light-show service. The legal basis is taking steps at your request prior to a possible contract (GDPR/RODO Art. 6(1)(b)).

Where it is stored

In a SQLite database inside our application container on a server in the EU (Hetzner, Finland). When notifications are enabled, a message saying only “a new lead arrived” is sent to the owner via Telegram — your personal details are not sent over Telegram. We use no analytics, advertising or non-essential cookies.

How long we keep it

Up to 12 months from our last contact about your enquiry, then it is deleted. Backups inherit the same period.

Music you upload

If you upload your own audio to run a light show, the file is used only to generate the light timeline and is deleted automatically right after — it is not stored, published, shared or linked to your identity, and the audio itself is never re-served (the show is lights only). You are responsible for holding the rights to any music you upload. This feature is off unless the operator enables it.

Your rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction or object to the processing, and withdraw any consent — email us and we will action it. You may also lodge a complaint with the Polish supervisory authority (UODO, uodo.gov.pl).
