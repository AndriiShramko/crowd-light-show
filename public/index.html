The control room

You drive it. The crowd is the light.

One laptop or tablet runs the whole show — from a single “GO” to VJ-grade live control, all in the browser.

The VJ pult — play the crowd by hand

Grab saturation, colour, brightness and flash and ride them under the music — like a VJ console, right in the browser. Open it fullscreen and mix with your fingers on a tablet.

  • Four touch layouts: faders, colour wheel, XY pad, big momentary pads
  • Intervene in a running preset, or go fully manual
  • Map a USB MIDI controller with WebMIDI

Presets, flash & big moments

Upload a track and the lights follow the music. Layer a separate camera-flash channel, fire one-tap synchronized fireworks, scroll a message across every phone, or hit BLACKOUT — the music keeps playing.

  • Pulse · Colour Waves · Rainbow Chase · Ocean, plus a wave that reads across the crowd
  • Five one-tap firework bursts, then the show resumes
  • Scrolling marquee, global mute, seek, blackout

Lock it to your colours

Restrict the whole crowd to a flag’s or a brand’s palette — pick colours on the wheel and everything snaps to the nearest one. Perfect for a product launch, a club night, or a team’s colours.

  • One-tap flag & brand palettes, or build your own on the wheel
  • Works with the presets or under full manual control

Plug into your VJ rig

Already run Resolume, TouchOSC or Bitfocus Companion? Drive the crowd’s phones from the console you already use, over OSC — a tiny bridge runs on your own laptop, no cloud lock-in.

  • OSC address map for presets, colour, flash, fireworks and blackout
  • Optional WebMIDI for a hardware fader/pad box

How it works

Three steps to a synced crowd

No rigging, no installs, no rehearsal. From laptop to lit-up room in minutes.

1

Upload & project

You upload your playlist on a laptop and project a QR code on screen — or print it on the tickets.

2

The crowd scans & joins

People point their phone camera at the QR and join in seconds — straight in the browser, no app needed.

3

You press GO

Hit GO and every phone flashes to the music, perfectly in sync — one light show across the whole room.
